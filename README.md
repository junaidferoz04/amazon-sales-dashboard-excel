# 🛒 Amazon Sales & Performance Analytics Dashboard

An interactive, end-to-end Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze transaction metrics, revenue across product categories and regions, customer payment patterns, and customer satisfaction levels.

## 🔑 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| Metric | Value | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Revenue** | `$640,114` | Cumulative revenue generated across all completed orders |
| **Total Orders** | `1,000` | Total number of recorded transaction orders |
| **Units Sold** | `3,019` | Aggregate volume of products sold |
| **Avg Rating** | `3.0 / 5.0` | Average customer review rating across categories |

---

## 💡 Key Visual Insights & Components

* **Category Revenue:** **Sports** (`$115,652`) and **Books** (`$112,368`) drive the highest total revenue among product categories.
* **Regional Distribution:** Sales revenue is balanced across global markets (**Asia**, **Europe**, **Middle East**, and **North America**), each generating around 23–26% of overall sales.
* **Payment Channels & Preference:** Includes deep-dive analysis into preferred payment methods (**UPI**, **Credit Cards**, **Debit Cards**, **Wallets**, **Cash on Delivery**) broken down by region and order frequency.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Customized dual-column side-by-side slicers for seamless filtering by **Month**, **Region**, and **Payment Method**.

---

## ⚙️ Data & Technical Details

* **Tool:** Microsoft Excel (PivotTables, PivotCharts, Custom Slicer Styles, Calculated Fields)
* **Dataset Size:** 1,000 transactional records across 13 attributes (`order_id`, `product_category`, `price`, `quantity_sold`, `customer_region`, `payment_method`, `rating`, `total_revenue`, etc.)
